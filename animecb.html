Anime Cookbook:
The taste of anime

Ever seen those delicious foods they prepare in anime? The BookCooks bring the taste of your favorite anime to you.

  • Print
  • Illustrator icons & graphics

Finding recipes and designing for print

This is a project I did in my first year of Communication & Multimedia Design in collaboration with Naïm van der Wal and Jeannet Welboren. All three of us are a fan of anime and were looking for a way to incorporate this into a design. At this point in our education, we were still learning about the principles of design.

Our first challenge was to find the right recipes. The next step was designing a lay-out for the book. We did research on other cook books and tested multiple lay-outs to see what works best. One of the most fun parts of this journey was finding the right style in typography, colors, icons, graphics and patterns. I put a lot of effort in creating an icon set and patterns that match te vibe. We were looking to incorporate drawn elements of anime and japanese culture into the design.

The best part of this project was receiving the actual printed version of the cookbook. It is a nice way to remember our early student days and see how far we've come from that moment.

Feel free to browse through the pages of the anime cookbook and maybe try out one of the recipes!
