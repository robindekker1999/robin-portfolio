Which rapper's dead had the most impact?

Ever noticed how streams skyrocket when a popular rapper dies and they suddenly become really popular? But who has had the biggest impact after they died?

  • Information design
  • Data design

Combining data with design and music

For this one-week project, I teamed up with my fellow students Ryan Hillert and Martijn Kooijman. We observed that streams of popular rappers increase exponentially after their death. This led us to wonder: which rapper's death has had the biggest impact?

Our first major challenge was collecting a significant amount of data. To get the necessary information, we compared the number of streams one day before and one day after the death of 10 different popular rappers who died in the last 10 years.

In addition to data on streams, we also gathered information on the most common causes of death for rappers, which we incorporated into our research. Using Andy Kirk's book on Data Visualization, we created a digital poster to showcase our findings. My primary focus during this project was designing the poster and ensuring all graphics maintained a consistent aesthetic.
